What is Judo?



Judo originates from the Japanese language, witch means "The gentle way". It is counted as a lot of thing for many different people. For some people it is a sport, for some it can be art or even a way of life!


Judo was founded in 1882 in Japan by Jigoro Kano. He modified dangerous techniques from jiu-jitsu, and developped Judo into a sport. He also gave Judo a physical education emphasis and a strong moral orientation. It is now the 2nd most practiced sport in the world.
